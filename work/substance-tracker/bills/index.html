The Four Substantive Bills

The four bills Bitcoin end users actually need.

Tax treatment of small transactions. Tax treatment of mining. Liability cover for open-source developers. Privacy guarantees for self-custody. Each item has been deprioritized by the industry trade associations as their attention shifted to broader market-structure legislation. These four are what's left of the substantive end-user agenda.
