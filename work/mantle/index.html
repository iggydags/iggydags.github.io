Mantle

real-estate transaction workspace · thesis to shipped product · vanilla HTML/CSS/JS

The transaction workspace for top-producing real-estate agents. Mantle owns the stretch between accepted offer and closing, the highest-stakes and least-tooled part of a deal: deadline and document tracking, closing-readiness, and client communication drafted in the agent's own voice. The signal is range under one hand: thesis, product architecture, feature design, competitive read, risk model, and build roadmap are all the same author, shipped as zero-framework single-file surfaces.

Independent project, live in production at mantle.us.com. The hub and data room are passphrase-gated; the working documents below are open. Every page is hand-built vanilla HTML/CSS/JS.
