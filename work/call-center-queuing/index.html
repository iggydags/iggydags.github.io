Call-Center Staffing

forecasting + queuing theory + simulation · Python

How many people staff an emergency dispatch line, hour by hour, to keep callers from hanging up? Answered three ways pointed at one question: forecast demand, solve for optimal staffing with heavy-traffic queuing theory, and validate with a from-scratch simulator.

  • QUEUING_SIMopen →Discrete-event queuing simulator built on a heapq event queue
